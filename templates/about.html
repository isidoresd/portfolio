About me

Currently studying Design Engineering at Middlesex University, where I am immersed in a project that looks at ways of improving a wheelchair to better support someone with tetraplegia. This involves utilising my design knowledge and Computer Aided Design skills.

I am also currently teaching myself programming with a specific interest in computer vision using both Python and C++. When not doing this, I enjoy learning Mandarin and reading Chinese classics and history along with some Japanese.

Some of the photos featured on this site are by myself and are an ongoing project that intends to capture the beautiful mundanity of London's peripheries.
