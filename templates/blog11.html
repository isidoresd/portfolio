11 Product Design Specification

01/03/21

This Product Design Specification (PDS) will outline any essential requirements that must be met are shown here with a D. Requirements that are non-essential but deemed desirable are shown here with a W.

These requirements are preliminary therefore subject to change.

No.  D/W Requirements  Description
Performance    
1 D Sufficiently support the
posture of someone with
tetraplegia.		 Apply ergonomic principles. Recognise the needs of a tetraplegic body.
2 W Extended degree of flexibility User requirements can
often very from person to
person. 
3 D Adaptive and responsive
according to stimuli		 Adapt to prolonged use in order to reduce health
problems
Environment      
4 D Weather resistant Chair may be used outside, therefore must be able to withstand this.
Life Expectancy    
5 D High durability Device will be used constantly, daily. Therefore it must be able to withstand this extended use.
Maintenance    
6 D Washable Should be easily
washable without dissassembling or removing any parts.
7 D Simple maintainance Any routine maintenance (adjustments etc.) should be easily undertaken with simple, commonly
available tools.
Size and weight     
8 D Finished design must not
weigh more than 10kg.
9 D Maximum width of 65cm  The chair needs to be
narrow enough to be used in restricted spaces.
Aesthetic      
10 W Improve visually upon
existing designs.		 Will be examined using
questionnaire
