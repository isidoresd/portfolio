12 Anthropometric Data

07/03/21

To design a chair that is suitably supportive, it is neccessary to understand the dimensions of the human body. Various standards agencies hold data anthropometric data but are unfortunately expensive to acquire. Fortunately, the US Army conducted extensive anthropometric studies on over 5000 members of its staff.

The averages for this data is shown below, split into male and female and listed with the corresponding area of the body the measurement is taken from. Some measurements have a number which corresponds to the diagram further down this page.
