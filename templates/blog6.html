06 Supervisor Meeting

04/02/21

Todays meeting was focused on a couple of possible avenues. They were designing supportive seating methods for someone with either flaccid or spastic tetraplegia or the eleviation of pressure ulcers. We examined existing solutions to pressure ulcer elleviation and found plenty that exist already. Two questions come to mind: how can the project be justified and how can the project be produced?

Discussed how I could analyse exisiting research and designs, a gaming chair for instance, to create a project that enables a better posture and possibly the reduction in the likelihood of developing pressure ulcers. Pressure cushions were another product that we examined before discussing how an arduino and pressure sensors could be used to integrate with a a chair for a dynamic seating position. A system such as this could be incorporated into a chair that measures pressure levels and adjusts itself whenever an area has experienced prolonged pressure, to relieve that area.

We then discussed the project format further. One point being that aims for the device itself should be formulated post literature review but pre development. Consider the layout of the blog itself for easier accessibility and once literature review is near completion, make it downloadable as in a Microsoft Word format.
